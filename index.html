Common questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Is ArdysaModsTools free?
Yes — the app and the ModsPack are completely free. Download the installer or the portable build and you're good to go. No sign-up, no paywalls.
How do I install mods?
Download and run ArdysaModsTools, let it detect your Dota 2 installation, then apply the ModsPack or pick skins per hero — the app handles the rest in one click.
Do mods still work after a Dota 2 update?
Game patches can reset modded files. When that happens, just re-apply your mods from the app — and keep an eye on What's New for updated ModsPack releases.
How do I uninstall or restore default skins?
The app can disable mods or restore Dota 2's original files at any time — no manual file digging required.
Are the mods visual only?
Yes. These are client-side cosmetic changes — only you see them, and gameplay is not affected.
Where can I get help?
The community lives on Discord — support, mod showcases, and ModsPack news all land there first.
